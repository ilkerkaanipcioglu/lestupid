# Les Travel Scenario Pack

## Scenario 1: Nairobi Geography Student Wants To Go To Turkiye

User:

- Kenyan citizen;
- University of Nairobi geography student;
- already has LesTupid identity;
- wants to go to Turkiye for a study visit, conference, internship, exchange,
  sponsor/mentor meeting or tourism.

### Screen Flow

1. `Trip Intent`
   - From: Nairobi, Kenya.
   - To: Istanbul/Ankara/Izmir, Turkiye.
   - Purpose: study visit, conference, exchange, internship, tourism or sponsor
     meeting.

2. `Official Visa Source`
   - Shows official Turkiye e-Visa/foreign ministry/embassy links.
   - Shows last checked date.
   - Warns about fake visa/payment sites.
   - Does not claim final visa eligibility without official verification.

3. `Document Checklist`
   - passport;
   - student proof;
   - invitation/acceptance/conference letter, if relevant;
   - accommodation proof;
   - funding proof;
   - travel insurance;
   - return/onward travel plan;
   - school contact and emergency contact.

4. `Stay And Safety`
   - dorm/hotel/hostel/family/host options;
   - verified vs unverified stay labels;
   - public transport route;
   - airport arrival plan;
   - safe first-night plan;
   - trusted contact handoff.

5. `Opportunity Layer`
   - Les Match: only opt-in mentor, sponsor, same-event or student group.
   - Les Commerce: stay, SIM, transport, insurance or gear offers with clear
     terms.
   - Les AI/KADRO: document checklist, email draft, itinerary and language help.
   - Les Certification: official-source and verified-place signals.
   - Les Contacts: private travel memory and trip people/places.

6. `Arrival Mode`
   - Airport mode;
   - campus mode;
   - cafe/food mode;
   - safe return mode;
   - clinic/help mode;
   - embassy/consulate info link, if needed.

### University Launch Extension

For a University of Nairobi student or new graduate, the travel card should not
stand alone. It connects to:

- Living CV: geography fieldwork, mapping, campus quests, creator work and
  safe gigs become reviewed CV signals.
- Safe income: tutoring, field mapping support, peer courier, event help,
  campus creator tours and DIY/product demos can earn money with clear scope.
- Mentor/sponsor: people discovery stays in Les Match and requires explicit
  opt-in from both sides.
- Trust: Les Certification can show domain-scoped proof without exposing full
  identity or private history.
- Adult context: 18+ dating or creator choices are separate, legal/consent-first
  and never framed as payment for sexual services.

## Scenario 2: Phuket Holiday Without Losing Money Or Safety

User:

- wants a fun holiday;
- does not want fake bookings, unsafe operators, budget traps, document trouble,
  aggressive nightlife upsells, unsafe transport or risky private meetups.

### Screen Flow

1. `Trip Budget Guard`
   - total budget;
   - daily burn rate;
   - return-ticket protection;
   - emergency reserve;
   - card/cash split.

2. `Stay Verification`
   - booking source;
   - address and arrival route;
   - check-in rules;
   - deposit/refund terms;
   - neighborhood safety notes;
   - report controls.

3. `Risk Briefing`
   - fake tour/operator warning;
   - transport overcharge warning;
   - passport/document custody warning;
   - medical/tattoo/wellness operator verification;
   - nightlife pressure warning;
   - unsafe private meetup warning.

4. `Safe Alternatives`
   - certified/verified operators where available;
   - public meeting points;
   - safe route and return;
   - trusted contact;
   - emergency card.

5. `Social Layer`
   - travel companion matching only through explicit Les Match opt-in;
   - no pressure, no hidden expectations, no adult-service marketplace;
   - report/block/mute controls visible.

## Product Position

Les Travel should feel like a careful local friend, not a travel ad. It can make
the trip exciting, but it must protect the user's money, documents, body,
privacy, time and return plan.
