# Les Match Creator Membership Policy

Les Match can support creator/fan and member/community matching, but it must not
turn people into paid placement objects or frame gifts, trips, money or access
as payment for sexual services.

## Product Shape

Allowed:

- creator to fan discovery;
- fan club or supporter membership;
- paid access to clearly described content, events, streams or communities;
- travel/date expectation prompts for 18+ users only;
- creator collaboration matching;
- sponsor/mentor/creator support matching;
- student/new graduate sponsor and mentor discovery where both sides opt in;
- adult creator content only where legal, age-gated and platform-approved.

Forbidden:

- escorting or paid sexual service arrangement;
- money, gifts, travel or access framed as sexual compensation;
- minors or school users in adult creator contexts;
- unlabeled paid ranking;
- private message import by default;
- hidden boosts;
- coercive scarcity, manipulation or dependency loops.

## Matching Rules

- Creator membership is opt-in for creator and fan.
- People are never exposed unless both sides have Les Match active.
- Paid membership suggestions must be labeled as paid/commercial.
- Organic compatibility and paid access are separate explanations.
- Rejection, mute, block and report controls are always visible.
- Adult creator matching requires 18+ mode, legal jurisdiction checks and human
  review before certification.

## Opportunity Types

| Opportunity | Example |
| --- | --- |
| `creator_membership_prompt` | Follow/support a local musician, filmmaker, educator, fitness creator or adult creator where legal |
| `fan_to_creator_match` | A fan likes this concert trail and may join the creator's backstage Q&A membership |
| `creator_collab_match` | Two creators can produce a quest, short film, route or event together |
| `sponsor_to_creator_match` | A sponsor may support a student, venue, artist or travel creator |
| `student_launch_match` | A student or new graduate finds opt-in mentors, sponsors, project teammates or verified opportunity hosts |
| `adult_expectation_prompt` | 18+ users clarify dating/travel boundaries; not payment for sexual services |

## Certification Requirements

- AI/adult/commercial labels are visible.
- Content and membership terms are clear before payment.
- Age gate is enforced where required.
- No sexual service marketplace behavior.
- Paid ranking does not override safety or consent.
- Creator can export members/content metadata where legally allowed.
